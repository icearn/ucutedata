# Spending Tracker

This is a simple spending tracker app that allows you to track your spending and see how much you have spent in a given time period.
It focus on receipts and expenses, and allows you to add and remove them as needed.

## Features

- Add and remove receipts and expenses, with the ability to upload a receipt image and extract the data from it, OCR scan and AI based.
- Automatically categorize receipts and expenses, with the ability to train the AI to recognize your receipts and expenses.
- tracking for your spending habits over time, for example what food category you spend the most on
- tracking for your transactions with open banking enabled, with the ability to import your transactions from your bank account.
- analyze your execersise and health based on your food and health expenses
- mark the favorite receipts and expenses
- mark the favorite shops and services
- mark the most expensive receipts and expenses
- give suggestions based on your spending habits, like what's the recent cheap deals.
- give suggestions based on your health and exercise habits, like any group by or compaign sales for these services.
- View your spending in a given time period
- Export your spending to a CSV file
- Import your spending from a CSV file
- View your spending in a pie chart split by category
- View your spending in a bar chart split by category
- View your spending in a table with all the details
- View your spending in a calendar on days
- View your spending in a map, marking the location of the shops and services    
- View your spending in a 3D pie chart, split by category
- View your spending in a 3D line chart, split by time, or category over a period of time
- View your spending in a 3D histogram, split by category
- View your spending in a 3D heat map, split by time
- View your spending in a 3D bubble map, split by category
- API for your spending data to other applications/services with your permission as oauth2

## Security

Data security and privacy is the key priority for this app. 
- All data is encrypted at rest and in transit.
- All data is stored in a secure database/format only on your device.
- All data is accessible only by you, and only with your permission.
- All data is deleted when you delete it.
- All data is deleted when you delete the app.
- All data is deleted when you delete your account.
- block chain and other secure data sharing can be enabled with your permission.

## Privacy
Camera used with permission for scanning receipts.
Geolocation maybe needed to collect the location of the shops and services.
